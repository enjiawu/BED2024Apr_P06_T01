Singapore Red Cross Society

Providing aid and relief to those in need

The Singapore Red Cross Society is dedicated to alleviating human suffering and protecting human dignity through humanitarian services and disaster relief efforts. Join us in our mission to provide aid and support to vulnerable communities.

Focus Areas

  • Disaster Response and Relief
  • Blood Donation
  • Community Services

Impact

Over the years, the Singapore Red Cross Society has provided crucial aid during disasters, collected thousands of units of blood, and supported numerous community programs aimed at improving the lives of those in need.

Leadership

Mr. Benjamin William

Mr. Benjamin William is the Secretary General and CEO of the Singapore Red Cross Society. He has extensive experience in humanitarian aid and disaster management.

Michael Chen

Michael Chen oversees our conservation programs and partnerships. He has a background in environmental science and has worked on conservation projects around the world.

Zero Waste SG

Empowering a zero-waste future

Zero Waste SG is a non-profit organization dedicated to promoting sustainable waste management practices in Singapore. Through education, advocacy, and community initiatives, we aim to reduce waste and support a circular economy. Join us in our journey towards a zero-waste future.

Cause Areas

  • Waste Reduction
  • Recycling & Upcycling
  • Public Education & Awareness

Impact

Zero Waste SG has engaged with thousands of individuals and businesses to promote waste reduction practices, conducted numerous workshops and campaigns, and successfully diverted tons of waste from landfills through recycling and upcycling initiatives.

Leadership

Eugene Tay

Eugene Tay is the founder and Executive Director of Zero Waste SG. With a background in environmental management, he is passionate about sustainability and has been a prominent advocate for zero-waste practices in Singapore.

Grace Chua

Grace Chua is the Program Director at Zero Waste SG. She oversees various community programs and initiatives aimed at reducing waste and promoting recycling. Her expertise lies in environmental education and community engagement.

WWF Singapore

Building a future where people live in harmony with nature

WWF Singapore is committed to protecting the world's most vulnerable species and ecosystems. Through conservation efforts, sustainable development programs, and public education, we strive to create a future where people and nature thrive together. Join us in our mission to safeguard the natural world.

Focus Areas

  • Wildlife Conservation
  • Climate Change Mitigation
  • Ocean Protection

Impact

WWF Singapore has played a key role in numerous conservation projects, including protecting endangered species, promoting sustainable practices, and engaging communities in environmental stewardship. Our efforts have helped conserve critical habitats and reduce human impact on the environment.

Leadership

R. Raghunathan

R. Raghunathan is the CEO of WWF Singapore. With a wealth of experience in environmental conservation and corporate sustainability, he leads the organization's strategic initiatives to protect nature and promote sustainable development.

Elaine Tan

Elaine Tan is the Conservation Director at WWF Singapore. She oversees various conservation programs and initiatives, focusing on biodiversity protection and sustainable resource management.
